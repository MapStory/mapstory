How It Works

Upload StoryLayers

Upload datasets as StoryLayers or, if you don't have data to upload, generate new StoryLayers from scratch.

Edit StoryLayers

Edit StoryLayers shared by the community to make them as complete and accurate as possible

Compose MapStories

Compose MapStories by combining StoryLayers with text, images and video and then share them with a global audience

Contribute to the global data commons

Put your data in a place where it can live accessible to all, in perpetuity, without being subject to commercial influence

Share your expertise through edits

Curate the accuracy and quality of StoryTellers by adding ratings, checking metadata, making comments and commiting version edits.

Bring your story to life

Combine StoryLayers with narrative elements like images, text and video to compose MapStories that explain how and why change occurs. No coding required.
